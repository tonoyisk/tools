Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! Our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can compress as many images as you need without any limitations.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can choose to keep the original format or convert to another format during compression.

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to minimize quality loss. You can adjust the compression level to find the perfect balance between file size and quality for your needs.
